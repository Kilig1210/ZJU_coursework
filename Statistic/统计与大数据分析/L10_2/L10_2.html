线性回归的误差估计#

对一组测量(\(X_i,Y_i\))值，认为Y与X符合线性关系

\[ Y = b_0+b_1X +e \]

  • 其中\(b_0+b_1X\)是需要确定的回归函数

  • 认为E(e)=0, Var(e)=\(\sigma^2\), 为随机因素

将回归函数的表达式改写为

\[ Y = \beta_0 + \beta_1 (X-\bar{X})\]

  • 被称为模型的中心化

\(\beta_0\)和\(\beta_1\)独立

(1)#\[\begin{align} \hat{\beta_0} &= \sum Y_i/n = \bar{Y} \\ \hat{\beta_1} &= \frac{\sum (X_i -\bar{X})Y_i}{S^2_X}\\ \text{var}(\hat\beta_0) &= \sigma^2/n\\ \text{var}(\hat\beta_1) &= \sigma^2/S^2_X \end{align}\]

转化为\(b_0\)和\(b_1\)

(2)#\[\begin{align} b_0 &= \beta_0 - \beta_1\bar{X}\\ b_1&= \beta_1\\ \text{var}(b_0) &= \text{var}(\beta_0) + \bar{X}^2\text{var}(\beta_1)\\ \text{var}(b_1) &= \text{var}(\beta_1) \end{align}\]

线性回归的几种误差（区间估计）#

  • 参数\(\beta_0\)和\(\beta_1\)的区间

  • \(f(x_i)\)的区间

  • 回归函数\(f(x)\)的区间

  • \(Y_i= f(x_i) + e_i \)的区间

参数\(\beta_0\)和\(\beta_1\)的区间#

  • 假设\(e_i\)服从正态分布

  • \(\hat\beta_{0,1}\)是\(\beta_{0,1}\)的无偏估计，服从正态分布，方差为\(\text{var}(\hat\beta_{0,1})\)，

(3)#\[\begin{align} \text{var}(\hat\beta_0) &= \sigma^2/n \\ \text{var}(\hat\beta_1) &= \sigma^2/S^2_X \end{align}\]

根据中心极限定理

\[(\hat\beta_0-\beta_0)/(\sigma/\sqrt{n}) \sim N(0,1)\]

  • 其中含有未知数\(\sigma\), \(\hat\sigma = \delta/\sqrt{n-2}\)

\[(\hat\beta_0-\beta_0)/(\hat\sigma/\sqrt{n}) \sim t_{n-2}\]

  • 用\(t_{n-2}(\alpha/2)\)代表p-value为\(\alpha\)的x取值

\(\beta_0\)的1-\(\alpha\)置信区间为

\[ [\hat\beta_0- \hat\sigma/\sqrt{n}t_{n-2}(\alpha/2),\hat\beta_0+ \hat\sigma/\sqrt{n}t_{n-2}(\alpha/2) ]\]

\(\beta_1\)的1-\(\alpha\)置信区间为

\[ [\hat\beta_1- \hat\sigma/S_X t_{n-2}(\alpha/2),\hat\beta_1+ \hat\sigma/S_X t_{n-2}(\alpha/2) ]\]

\(f(x_i)\)的区间#

(4)#\[\begin{align} \text{var}(\hat\beta_0) &= \sigma^2/n \\ \text{var}(\hat\beta_1) &= \sigma^2/S^2_X \end{align}\]

对于函数\(f(x)\)的每一个点 \(f(x_i)\)

\[f(x_i) = \beta_0+\beta_1(x_i-\bar{x})\]
\[\hat{f}(x_i) = \hat{\beta_0}+\hat{\beta_1}(x_i-\bar{x})\]

  • \(\hat{\beta}_{0,1}\)符合正态分布，故\(\hat{f}(x_i)\)符合正态分布

(5)#\[\begin{align} v= \text{var}(\hat{f}(x_i)) &= \text{var}(\hat{\beta_0}+ \hat{\beta_1}(x_i-\bar{x})) \\ &= \sigma^2[1/n+(x-\bar{x})^2/S^2_X] \end{align}\]

估计\(f(x_i)\)的区间

\[(f(x_i)-\hat f(x_i) )/\sqrt{v} \sim N (0,1)\]

用\(\hat{\sigma}\)代替\(\sigma\)

\[(f(x_i)-\hat f(x_i) )/[\hat\sigma(1/n+(x-\bar{x})^2/S^2_X)^{1/2}]\sim t_{n-2}\]

则 \(f(x_i)\)的1-\(\alpha\)区间为

\[ [\hat f(x_i) \pm \hat\sigma(1/n+(x-\bar{x})^2/S^2_X)^{1/2} t_{n-2}(\alpha/2)]\]

  • \(f(x_i)\)的方差与\(x_i\)的位置有关，离平均值越近，方差越小

  • n趋于无穷大时，1/n趋于0, \((x-\bar{x})^2/S^2_X\)趋于0，方差趋于0

多变量的置信区间#

  • \(\beta_0\)和\(\beta_1\)独立，各自为正态分布，方差分别为\(\sigma^2(\beta_0),\sigma^2(\beta_1\))

  • 它们的联合分布为二维正态分布

\[ f(x,y) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{(2\pi)^k \det \Sigma}} \exp{\bigl(-1/2 (x-\mu)^\intercal \Sigma ^{-1} (x-\mu)\bigl)}\]
\[\begin{split}\Sigma= \begin{bmatrix} \sigma_1^2 & \rho \sigma_1\sigma_2 \\ \rho \sigma_1\sigma_2 & \sigma_2^2 \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

  • 二维正态分布的\(\alpha\)置信区间不等于各自一维置信区间构成的矩形空间

  • 对于一维的正态分布x，有\((x-\mu)^2/\sigma^2\)符合\(\chi^2_1\)分布

  • 对于p维正态分布的一组向量X，有

\[ (X-\mu)^\intercal \Sigma^{-1}(X-\mu) \sim \chi^2_{p}\]

  • 当用测量值来估计\(\mu\)时,\(\Sigma\)已知

\[ (\bar{X}-\mu)^\intercal \Sigma^{-1}(\bar{X}-\mu) \sim \chi^2_{p}\]

  • \(\Sigma\)未知，用S/(n-p)代替\(\Sigma\),

\[ \frac{n(n-p)}{p(n-1)} (\bar{X}-\mu)^\intercal S^{-1}(\bar{X}-\mu) \sim F_{p,n-p}\]

  • 对于二维正态分布, 用\(\hat\Sigma\)=S/(n-2)来估计\(\Sigma\)

\[ \frac{n}{2(n-1)} (\bar{X}-\mu)^\intercal \hat{\Sigma}^{-1}(\bar{X}-\mu) \sim F_{2,n-2}\]
\[ (\hat\beta_0-\beta_0)^2/\sigma^2(\beta_0)+(\hat\beta_1-\beta_1)^2/\sigma^2(\beta_1) < 2F_{2,n-2}(0.05)\]

Y的预测区间#

\[ Y = f(x)+e \]
\[ \hat {Y}_i = \hat {f}(x_i)+\hat e = \hat {f}(x_i)\]
(6)#\[\begin{align} \text{Var} (Y_i) &= \text{Var}(e) + \text{Var}(\hat f(x_i))\\ &= \sigma^2 (1+1/n+(x-\bar{x})^2/S^2_X) \end{align}\]

用\(\hat{\sigma}\)代替\(\sigma\)

\[(\hat {Y}_i - Y_i)/ [\sigma^2 (1+1/n+(x-\bar{x})^2/S^2_X)] \sim t_{n-2}\]

\(Y_i\)的置信区间由\(t_{n-2}(\alpha/2)\)得到

  • 与\(f(x_i)\)相比，\(Y_i\)的方差多了一项\(\sigma^2\)

  • 当n趋于无穷时，\(Y_i\)的方差趋于\(\sigma^2\)

线性回归的假设检验#

  • 通常关心两个变量之间是否相关，即\(\beta_1\)是否为0

  • 假设\(H_0\):\(\beta_1=0\), \(|\hat\beta_1 -0 | \leq t_{n-2}(0.025)\sigma_{\beta_1}\)

  • p-value: \(P(t_{n-2}(|x|>\hat\beta_1/\sigma_{\beta_1})) = 2(1-CDF(t_{n-2}(\hat\beta_1/\sigma_{\beta_1})) )\)

pval=2*(1-st.t(n-2).cdf(beta1/std_beta1))
r = st.pearsonr(y,x)
print(r,pval)

回归函数的逆转使用#

  • \(Y= b_0+b_1X\) 能否推出\(X=c_0+c_1Y\)?

  • \(X = (Y-b_0)/b_1\)

  • \(c_0 = -b_0/b_1, c_1 = 1/b_1\)
